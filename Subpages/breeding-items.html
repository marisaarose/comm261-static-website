Breeding Items

The Pokémon games have some breeding items in the most recent games that help make it significantly easier to hatch Pokémon with perfect IVs with a favorable nature.

Item Purpose

Destiny Knot		 When a Pokemon holding Destiny Knot lays an egg, 5 out of 12 of the parent Pokemon's IVs will be passed down to the child Pokemon.

Everstone		 An item to be held by a Pokemon. A Pokemon holding this peculiar stone is prevented from evolving. When a Pokemon holds an Everstone while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Nature to its offspring.

Power Anklet		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's Speed stat to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Anklet while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Speed IV to its offspring.

Power Band		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's Sp. Def stat to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Band while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Sp. Def IV to its offspring.

Power Belt		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's Defense stat to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Belt while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Defense IV to its offspring.

Power Bracer		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's Attack stat to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Bracer while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Attack IV to its offspring.

Power Lens		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's Sp. Atk stat to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Lens while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its Sp. Atk IV to its offspring.

Power Weight		 An item to be held by a Pokémon. It reduces Speed but allows the holder's max HP to grow more after battling. When a Pokemon holds a Power Weight while breeding, the Pokemon will pass on its HP IV to its offspring.
